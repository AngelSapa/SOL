About Stewards of the Land (SOL)

SOL is a project born out of love for both people and nature. It aims to address the needs of underserved communities, particularly in urban environments where resources are scarce and expensive. By integrating sustainable living practices, SOL offers a path toward reducing the everyday struggles of low-income families without drastic reforms to the market or legislative systems.

Our Vision

We envision a community where access to land stewardship, affordable housing, nutritious foods, alternative healing, and artistic expression are available to all. Our work promotes teamwork, cultural revival, and sustainable living practices to help individuals and communities thrive.
