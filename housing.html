Housing and Affordable Living

Housing is a critical part of ensuring a sustainable future. At SOL, we focus on creating affordable, eco-friendly housing solutions that empower communities to take control of their living spaces. We work with natural materials, like cob, to build homes that are both cost-effective and environmentally friendly.

Sustainable Building

Our approach to housing emphasizes the use of natural and locally-sourced materials. Cob building is one of the many techniques we teach in workshops to create durable, aesthetically pleasing homes at a fraction of the cost of traditional construction.

Community Ownership

We believe in collective ownership of land and housing. Our model promotes stewardship, where communities take care of the land, making housing an asset for all members rather than a financial burden for individuals.
