Community Services

Community services are at the heart of SOL's mission. We strive to provide access to education, vocational training, and other essential resources that help individuals and families improve their quality of life. Our programs are designed to build skills, foster community connections, and create self-sustaining, empowered communities.

Education and Training

We offer hands-on workshops and educational programs that cover a variety of topics including housing construction, sustainable farming, health and wellness, and technical skills. These programs are aimed at providing people with the tools they need to be self-reliant and thrive in their communities.

Support Networks

SOL believes in the power of community to uplift and support one another. We create networks where people can share resources, knowledge, and emotional support, fostering a sense of unity and resilience in the face of social and economic challenges.
